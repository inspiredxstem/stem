About InspiredxSTEM

On November 8th, Brooklyn High School for Law and Technology will be opening its doors to anyone interested in technology and its plethora of applications. Technology is our future, and in order to continue innovating, advancing, and evolving, more people need to be educated in the field.

As such, InspiredxSTEM brings technology to our community through workshops and a hackathon, an event where people create awesome project during the day, in an effort to inspire more students, teachers, and parents to utilize the rapidly expanding technological world
